About Me

I am a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor at Great Bay University. Before that, I was a Research Scientist at Meta.
I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from The Ohio State University under the supervision of Dr. Yang Wang.

I am currently seeking highly motivated PhD students (for Spring 2027 and Fall 2027), research assistants, visiting students, and two postdoctoral researchers to join my group.
Experience with distributed systems and OS kernels is preferred.
If you are interested, please feel free to contact me with your CV and transcripts.
