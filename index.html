Biography

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor at the School of Information Science and Technology, Great Bay University.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from The Ohio State University in 2021, advised by Dr. Yang Wang. I also hold an M.S. from Auburn University, an M.E. from Harbin Institute of Technology, and a B.E. from Central South University.

Join My Research Group

I am actively recruiting PhD Students (Spring 2027, Fall 2027), Research Assistants, Visiting Students, and Postdoctoral Researchers.

If you are interested in research on AI/ML Systems, Performance Analysis, Recommender Systems, Distributed Systems, or Operating Systems, please feel free to contact me via email!

Research Interests

My research focuses on AI/ML systems, performance analysis, and large-scale distributed systems. I am currently working on LLM inference optimization, performance analysis, and AI for systems.
